About The Movie

‘Along for the Telluride’ is about two strangers named Olive and Arden who are forced to share a ride to Telluride Colorado when there is only one (all wheel drive) car left to rent. Along the way they make various stops and end up learning more about each other than they anticipated.

‘Along for the Telluride’ trata sobre dos extraños llamados Olive y Arden que se ven obligados a compartir un viaje a Telluride Colorado cuando solo queda un automóvil (con tracción total) para alquilar. En el camino hacen varias paradas y terminan aprendiendo más el uno del otro de lo que esperaban.

The Origin Story

Finding the hallmark channel was a pivotal moment in our relationship where we quickly realized that instead of scrolling endlessly for a good movie, we could turn to this channel for endless enjoyment of painfully cheesy love stories that span endless time periods and seasons. Hallmark movies be like.. just when you think the small town girl is going to visit her home town for only one day after years of running a huge business and being too busy with work, she finds a reason to stay with her first true love that’s been waiting for her his whole life since she left… and built their dream home!!

Follow us on Instagram

Our plan to visit Telluride came from us googling top hallmark towns, where we could live out our dreams of pretending we were the main characters of the worst and cheesiest love story ever. This sparked the idea of filming a short film for fun and while we knew we wanted to film something, we had no script and no plan when we started filming in Telluride.
